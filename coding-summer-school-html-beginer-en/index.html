Our Solar System

Our Solar System has eight planets. Four Terrestrial. Four Jovian. The Terrestrial planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars. The Terrestrial planets have a solid surface, have no ring systems and have no, or just a few moons. The Jovian planets are Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune. The Jovian planets support ring systems, have multiple moons and don't have a solid surface.
